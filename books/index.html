Book notes

This page used to be where I kept notes and comments from books I’ve read. Sadly, I’ve stopped publishing these notes due to duplication of work (I also write them out by hand to index cards and keep them in a commonplace book). Maybe one day I’ll resurrect my notes, but for now, I write about what I learn from books in my posts.
