What I’m doing now

I’m working on my new business yvant.io. Founded in March 2020 with my co-founder Iván Perdomo, Yvant is a DevOps consultancy that helps its clients run on less, produce more and deliver faster. We’ve been busy with setup activities and working with our first client. Things are going well and I’m excited for what’s in store, not only for us but also for our clients.

I have also become a teacher (unofficially). I now spend my mornings with my kids reading, doing maths, literacy, topic work and P.E. I also have them learning to touch type.

I’m enjoying the quiet life and quality time with my family that the COVID-19 pandemic has given us. I feel closer to my kids than ever before. We don’t have to make decisions about what to do or where to go. I’m catching up with my family and friends more than ever on video calls. Movies, reading, exercise, laying about in the garden. It’s junk time at its finest.
