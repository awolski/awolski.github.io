What I’m doing now

The UK is back in strict lockdown due to soaring Covid cases and, sadly, rising deaths. Like so many others I’m juggling home school and work. We’re trying to keep safe and at the same time remain connected with friends and family.

I’m working on turning Yvant into a Co-op.

I’m reading Bodyguard of Lies by Anthony Cave Brown, a behemoth of a book, and certainly the largest I’ve ever read.

I’m reconnecting. I removed myself from social media years ago, so it’s harder to stay in contact with friends and family. I’m sending lots of emails to catch up on where people are and how they’re doing.
