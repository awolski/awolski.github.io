What I’m doing now

The UK is back in strict lockdown due to soaring Covid cases and, sadly, rising deaths. So like so many others I am juggling home school and work. We’re trying to keep safe but at the same time keeping connections with friends and family open.

I’m reading. The Great Influenza by Jonh M. Barry (incredible) and Atomic Habits (again) by James Clear.

I’m reconnecting. Having removed myself from social media years ago it’s harder to stay in contact with friends and family than it used to be. I’ve never been particularly good staying in touch anyway. But I’m using what Atomic Habits teaches me to build my “stay in contact” muscles.

Updated 7th January 2021.
